Team Registration

To ensure fair evaluation and final result verification, all participating teams must complete the team registration form before making official submissions.

1

Register Your Team

Only the team leader needs to submit the form. Please include the real name, Codabench username, and email address of every team member.

Open registration form in a new tab

2

Submit on Codabench

If Codabench supports team or organization submission, please submit using the registered team name. If the platform cannot enforce team submission, final results will be audited according to the registration form.

Online Team Registration Form Open in new tab

Submission and Audit Rules

Required: Each team submits the registration form only once.
Required: Each person may belong to only one registered team.
Required: Each Codabench account may appear in only one team record.
Audit: If multiple members of one team submit results, the best valid score is treated as the official team score.
Audit: If one person appears in multiple teams, organizers may keep only one valid team association after review.
Updates: Team information changes must be requested by email before the final deadline.

Registration Updates

If a team needs to change members or correct account information, the team leader should email the organizers with the team name, leader contact, requested change, and reason for the change.
