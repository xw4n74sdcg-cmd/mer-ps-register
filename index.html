Team Registration and Submission Audit

MER-PS Challenge

Register your team before official submissions so that final results can be audited fairly and consistently.

Register Team Open Codabench

Overview

Each participating team should complete the team registration form once. The registration information will be used to verify team membership, Codabench accounts, submission ownership, and final ranking eligibility.

Only the team leader needs to submit the form. Every member must provide a real name, Codabench username, and email address.

One Person, One Team

Each participant may belong to only one registered team for MER-PS.

Registration-Based Audit

Final scores and eligibility will be checked against the submitted team information.

Email-Based Updates

Membership or account changes should be requested by email before the final deadline.
